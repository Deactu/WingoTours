Wine, gastronomy, culture and fun!

As we know, in the modern world travel is becoming more and more popular as an amusement for a people. But what we are most offered are tours that are full of information, from which we probably remember only 10% on the next day. Therefore, our approach is different - we provide information very neatly, carefully and dosedly to our guests.

Despite the fact that we have a specific program - not one tour is the same because our guests has an opportunity to be more tour participants but not just listeners. In this, we are greatly helped by individual communication with them, the games that we have invented for our project and, of course, the alcohol that we try while we ride around the city. We sell emotions and get them in return. Also, we must not forget that tourism research fully acknowledges the role of food and wine as contemporary cultural resources.

For all this, we have created a wine tasting bus, which allows people to explore the city sights while enjoying different drinks right on a special bus, with a feast inside. This is a mobile mini-bar where you can have fun while you move around the city. By this way, we said a new word in the field of tourism.

Wine Bus Service

combines 3 different products together:

  • Wine and gastro tour.
  • City tour.
  • Entertainment program.

During the tree hours visitors have the opportunity to get all these services in a short time, very efficiently.

Wine tasting tour by wine bus around the city

Excursion is unique due to the fact that wine tasting takes place right on board of the bus, while guests drive around the city, enjoy the architecture and atmosphere. During the tour, you will have an opportunity to taste 5 types of wine, two types of wine guests will taste on the bus board, on the way to the two different wine cellars, and another three in the cellars we are visiting. After the cellar guests will walk along the old streets and visit colorful courtyards.

    The trip is conducted on three languages: Georgian, English and Russian.
  • Maximum amount of guests are 14 people.
  • Tour duration - 3 hour.
  • Tour starting time - 17:00 and 20:00.

Wine tasting tour includes:

  • Wine tasting on wine bus board, three stops: first in an old cellar, second stop in a modern cellar, third stop - walking route of amazing attractions of the city.
  • Guide service.
  • Transportation.
  • 5 types of wine.
  • 2 type of chacha.
  • Cheese and sweets.
  • Dinner on request.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide safe, professional, good taste and reasonably priced sightseeing services to our customers.

We intend to fill a niche in Europe sightseeing and Enotourism market, that is unmet at this time, because it's our exclusive touristic product, we will show customer places in the cuty that were not shown before, as well as highlight the sides that are known to millions of visitors.

We never skimp on a safety and quality of our customer service. We always have and promote fun tours, so that when customers leave us, they remember the good times they had, and often recommend us to their friends and relatives. We offer a very friendly and courteus service - from the moment we meet them until the time to say goodbye to them.

It is very important to our company to support the ecology of our planet through the clean energy. So for the near future we are going to use only electric transport for our tours.

About us

Project "Wine Bus" is a brainchild of Giorgy Matsaberidze and Sofia Babluani, who was inspired to launch it after many years planning countless trips for customers in Georgia.

We were always seeking a new way of travel and adventure. The main idea was to make a trip joyful, delicious, interesting and curious. The project has existed since 2018 and despite the fact that from the beginning it was an experimental startup - just in a few months the product has became very interesting and attractive. We can confidently say that the experiment was a success.

After 4 successful years of the project's existence we decided to implement our project in different cities of Europe with the greatest numbers of visitors. First of all, we focus on countries that have a high culture of drinking wine and other alcoholic beverages.

We consider Spain as the first country after Georgia. We intend to move to Spain in order to use our experience for a correct and efficient implementation of our plan, and fully participate in company management there.
